Salon Welcome & Appointments

Welcome to TALON LUXE. We are committed to providing a professional, relaxing, and luxury beauty experience for every client.

  • Appointments can be booked online via website or Instagram.
  • A confirmation message will be sent prior to your appointment.
  • Please arrive 10-15 minutes early. Late arrivals may result in reduced treatment time.

Deposit Policy

A non-refundable deposit is required to secure all appointments.

  • Deposits go toward your final treatment cost.
  • Appointments without deposits won't be accepted.
  • Transferable only if notice is provided within the required timeframe.

Cancellation & Rescheduling

Requests must be made at least 24 hours before your appointment.

  • Late cancellations result in deposit forfeiture.
  • Same-day cancellations may be charged a fee.
  • Repeated no-shows will require full upfront payment.

Health, Safety & Hygiene

Your health is our priority. Please inform us of allergies, pregnancy, or medical conditions.

  • All tools are sanitized after every single use.
  • Disposable items are used where required.
  • We reserve the right to refuse service for disrespectful behavior or health concerns.

Payment & Refunds

We accept EFT and approved digital payment methods. Full payment is required immediately after services.

  • Refunds are not provided once services are completed.
  • Contact us within 48 hours for any concerns.
